St Mary’s eves win u-14 basketball tournament



The St Mary's School girls team representing Pune Division won the Inter Divisional Maharashtra State Basketball Championship at Yevatmal for the third consecutive year. The team led by national player Prerana Sheth won the Finals by defeating Mumbai Division by a score of 47 – 12, said a press release. The top scorers for Pune were Prerana Sheth 14 points, Krishna Shahane 12 points and Simran Ramesh 10 points. Rutuja Kulkarni with 6 points was the top scorer for Mumbai. On the way to the finals St. Mary's - Pune Girls defeated Nashik with a score of 50–3 in Quarter Finals and in Semi Finals they defeated Latur with a score 41-1. Prerana Sheth, Krishna Shahane and Simran Ramesh will represent Maharashtra and try retain the National Championship won last year by Maharashtra, the release said.

  • ST. MARY’S SCHOOL INTER SCHOOL ENGLISH DEBATE -T.K. RAO SAHIB KURUP TROPHY

    “Responsible Journalism Will Help in the Development of a Close- Knit Society” – an important topic in today’s day and age was the motion for the annual Rao Sahib T.K Kurup Trophy which was held on 20thJuly, 2018. Participants from eleven schools eloquently presented their views on this crucial topic. Our school was represented by Nandini Ghosh, who spoke for the motion and Vanshaja Mishra who spoke against the motion. The three eminent judges after a prolonged deliberation awarded BISHOP’S SCHOOL, CAMP, the BEST SCHOOL. The SECOND BEST SCHOOL was awarded to ARMY PUBLIC SCHOOL.

  • INTER HOUSE HINDI DEBATE

    The Inter house Hindi debate was held on the 22nd of June, 2018. The topic for the debate was “SAHKARMIYO KI AAPSI PRATISPARDHA SARVAANGEEN VIKAAS MEIN SAHAAYAK HAIN.” The participants put forward their opinions very convincingly on whether or not, competition and rivalry helps in the overall development of a person. Each speaker was interesting, coherent and made a connect with the audience. The debate was brought to a close with everybody being able to share their views and ideas with the audience.
